Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% marks in Intermediate/FA/FSc (Pre-Engineering)
  • Passed Entry Test (MUET-ET) with minimum 50% score
  • Valid CNIC/B-Form
  • No Objection Certificate for foreign qualifications
  • Medical fitness certificate from recognized hospital
  • Six passport-sized photographs
Additional department-specific requirements may apply. Please check department pages for details.
